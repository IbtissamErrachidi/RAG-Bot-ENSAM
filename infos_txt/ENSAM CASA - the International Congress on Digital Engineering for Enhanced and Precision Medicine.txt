Dear researchers,
We are pleased to invite you to attend the
DeepMED Congress 2025
, the
International Congress on Digital Engineering for Enhanced and Precision Medicine
, taking place in
Casablanca, Morocco, on May 22-23, 2025
.
https://deepmedcongress.com/
The
DeepMED Congress
is a leading event dedicated to technological and digital innovations in healthcare. It brings together researchers, healthcare professionals, engineers, and industry experts to discuss and explore groundbreaking advancements that are transforming medical practices and shaping the future of healthcare.
Topics and Scope
We welcome contributions in the field of digital engineering applied to medicine, including but not limited to:
Digital Engineering and Precision Medicine
Artificial Intelligence and Big Data in Medicine
Advanced Medical Imaging and Diagnostic Technologies
Digital Innovations in Dental Care
Health 4.0 and Digital Transformation
Cybersecurity and Data Privacy in Medical Systems
Augmented and Virtual Reality for Medical Training
Medical Robotics and Automation of Care

We look forward to your participation!
